Sheffield Genomics

{% include carousel_home.html %} The University of Sheffield is home to a wide range of cutting-edge 'omics equipment that researchers can access. We also have contacts at leading sequencing vendors, and will liaise with you to find the most appropriate solution for your sequencing needs. You will find links to various facilities around the campus, but please use our central contact in the first instance

Enquiry Form

Requests for a free consultation can be made via our central enquiry system. This is monitored by Genomics staff across the campus, so we will ensure the appropriate expertise (which may involve individuals from multiple teams) will be available to help you

Bioinformatics

Analysis support for your Genomics projects can be provided by Sheffield Bioinformatics Core. We can contact them on your behalf and involve them early in experimental design process. They can also be contacted for other analysis queries.
